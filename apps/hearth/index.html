Hearth

Static content for the Ember Catholic prayer app. The hearth sustains the ember.

  • hearth/v2/catalog.json — corpus catalog (kind-prefixed ids → blob hashes)
  • hearth/v2/blobs/{ab}/{cd}/{hash} — content-addressed blob store (prayers, practices, chapters, books, collections, propers)
  • hearth/v2/bible/drb/ — Douay-Rheims Bible (73 books)
  • hearth/v2/catechism/ — Catechism of the Catholic Church
  • hearth/v2/lectionary/ — OF lectionary data
  • hearth/v2/liturgical/ — liturgical calendar data
  • hearth/v2/propers/ — EF Mass propers (tempora + sancti)
  • hearth/v2/saints/ — Saint images (PNG + WebP)
