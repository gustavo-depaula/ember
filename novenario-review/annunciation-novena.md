# Annunciation — fidelity comparison

## Ember (this app) — en-US
**Opening prayer:** O Word of God, who at the angel's message became flesh in the most pure womb of the Virgin Mary, come and be born anew in my heart. As you found in her a willing dwelling, prepare in me a place to receive you. Amen.

**Day 1 — Day 1: The angel sent to Nazareth:** God sent His messenger not to a palace but to an obscure town and a poor maiden betrothed to a carpenter. The greatest event of history began in hiddenness and silence.  _(Intention: For the grace to seek God in the hidden and the humble.)_

**Day 2 — Day 2: Full of grace:** Gabriel greeted Mary as 'full of grace,' the Lord already with her before she had spoken a word. Her whole soul had been prepared from the first moment to be God's dwelling.  _(Intention: That God's grace may take fuller possession of my heart.)_

**Day 3 — Day 3: She was troubled:** Mary was troubled at the greeting and pondered what it might mean, neither rushing nor refusing. Her thoughtful silence shows a soul attentive to the ways of God.  _(Intention: For the grace to ponder God's word before acting.)_

**Day 4 — Day 4: 'How can this be?':** Mary asked how this could be, not in doubt but in a faith that seeks understanding. She wished only to know God's will so that she might fulfill it perfectly.  _(Intention: For a faith that trusts even what it cannot understand.)_

**Day 5 — Day 5: The Holy Spirit overshadows her:** The angel promised that the Holy Spirit would come upon her and the power of the Most High overshadow her. What was impossible to nature, God would accomplish by His own power.  _(Intention: That I may trust nothing is impossible to God.)_

**Day 6 — Day 6: 'Behold the handmaid of the Lord':** Mary called herself the handmaid of the Lord, claiming no title but service. In her, greatness and lowliness met, for the Mother of God wished only to be God's servant.  _(Intention: For the grace to serve God without seeking honor.)_

**Day 7 — Day 7: The fiat:** With 'Be it done unto me according to your word,' Mary gave the consent for which all creation waited. Her free 'yes' undid the disobedience of Eve and opened the door of salvation.  _(Intention: For the courage to say my own 'yes' to God's will.)_

**Day 8 — Day 8: The Word made flesh:** At her word the eternal Son took our flesh and made His home within her. Heaven came down to earth, and the Creator was carried by His creature.  _(Intention: In adoration of the mystery of God-with-us.)_

**Day 9 — Day 9: Mary's haste to serve:** Bearing the Lord within her, Mary rose and went in haste to serve her cousin Elizabeth. The first act of the Incarnate Word's presence was a journey of charity.  _(Intention: That, carrying Christ within me, I may hasten to serve others.)_

**Closing prayer:** Pour forth, we beseech you, O Lord, your grace into my heart, that as I have known the Incarnation of your Son by the message of an angel, so by His Passion and Cross I may be brought to the glory of His resurrection. Through Mary's fiat, grant the grace I ask in this novena. Amen.

_(A pt-BR version also exists in the app.)_

## Fidelity note
Ember keeps the traditional frame of this novena — the March 16–24 dating and the closing collect "Pour forth, we beseech you, O Lord, your grace into our hearts," which both sources also use — so the structural anchor and the most distinctive prayer are faithful. The sources (FishEaters and PrayMoreNovenas) share a single fixed daily prayer built around the "O most holy Virgin Mary" invocation and the Angelus versicles; Ember instead replaces this with nine original day-by-day meditations and intentions tracing the Annunciation scene (Gabriel sent, full of grace, the fiat, the Visitation), plus its own composed opening prayer. This is a deliberate paraphrase/recomposition rather than a reproduction: the wording of the daily prayers is Ember's own, and the closing collect is lightly modernized ("your" for "Thy") and given a novena petition tail ("Through Mary's fiat, grant the grace I ask"). Faithful in spirit, dating, and the signature collect; substantially original in its meditative daily content.
