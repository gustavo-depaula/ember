# Guardian Angels — fidelity comparison

## Ember (this app) — en-US
**Opening prayer:** Holy Guardian Angel, given to me by God's tender love to be my companion and guide, I greet you with gratitude. Watch over me through this novena, keep me close to God, and never let me stray from His side. Amen.

**Day 1 — A companion for every soul:** God in His goodness assigns to each person a guardian angel to accompany them from birth to death. This is no distant doctrine but a tender truth: I am never truly alone, for a pure spirit walks beside me.  _(Intention: For a living awareness of my Guardian Angel's presence each day.)_  (every day)

**Day 2 — Wonder at God's love:** That God should appoint a glorious spirit to watch over so small a creature is a marvel of His fatherly care. The Guardian Angel reveals how precious each soul is in the eyes of its Maker.  _(Intention: For the grace of wonder and gratitude at God's loving care.)_  (every day)

**Day 3 — Protection from harm:** The Psalm promises that the angels will bear us up lest we dash our foot against a stone. Our Guardian Angel shields us from dangers seen and unseen, often turning aside evils we will never know in this life.  _(Intention: For protection from every danger of body and soul.)_  (every day)

**Day 4 — A guide in temptation:** When the enemy whispers to lead us into sin, our Guardian Angel inspires good thoughts and recalls us to the path of virtue. He counters the tempter not with force upon our will but with gentle promptings of grace.  _(Intention: For the grace to heed my Angel's promptings and resist temptation.)_  (every day)

**Day 5 — A light for the mind:** The old prayer asks the Angel to enlighten and to guide, for he helps us see truth and choose wisely. In confusion and doubt we may ask his help to think clearly and to discern the will of God.  _(Intention: For light and wisdom to discern God's will in my decisions.)_  (every day)

**Day 6 — A friend in loneliness:** In hours of sorrow or solitude, when no human voice can reach us, our Angel remains near in faithful silence. He is a true friend who never tires, never sleeps, and never abandons the soul entrusted to him.  _(Intention: For comfort and companionship in times of loneliness and grief.)_  (every day)

**Day 7 — Carrier of our prayers:** Scripture shows the angels offering the prayers of the saints before God like fragrant incense. Our Guardian Angel joins his pure worship to our halting prayers and bears them upward to the throne of grace.  _(Intention: For the grace to pray more fervently with my Angel's help.)_  (every day)

**Day 8 — Defender at the hour of death:** At life's end the Guardian Angel stands by us, defending us from the enemy and bearing the faithful soul toward God. We may entrust to him now our final passage, that he lead us through that last darkness into light.  _(Intention: For my Angel's protection at the hour of my death.)_  (every day)

**Day 9 — Toward the vision of God:** The Guardian Angel's whole task is to bring his charge safely to the face of God, which he himself beholds without ceasing. His friendship now is a foretaste of the eternal company of heaven that he longs to share with us.  _(Intention: For the grace to reach heaven one day in the company of my Angel.)_  (every day)

**Closing prayer:** My faithful Guardian Angel, carry my prayers before the throne of God and obtain for me the grace I now implore. Enlighten, guard, rule, and guide me, that one day I may praise God with you forever. Amen.

_(A pt-BR version also exists in the app.)_

## Source — FishEaters
FishEaters carries a Novena to the Guardian Angel (anchor "guardianangel"), prayed Sep 23–Oct 1. It uses one fixed daily prayer beginning "O holy angel, whom God, by the effect of His goodness and His tender regard for my welfare, has charged with the care of my conduct…" — asking for continued charitable care, defense against adversaries, protection from occasions of sin, grace to follow the angel's holy inspirations, and especially protection "at the hour of my death… until thou hast conducted me into the presence of my Creator," with a personal intention inserted.

## Source — PrayMoreNovenas
PrayMoreNovenas opens each day with the Sign of the Cross and a fixed invocation ("Loving God, you are so good that you gave me a Guardian Angel to protect my body and my soul"), then assigns each day a different role for the angel — Counselor, Defender, Consoler, Teacher, Witness, Helper, Intercessor, Guide, Light — with a matching request, and closes "My guardian angel, [role], [pray for me]!"

## Fidelity note
Ember's novena is an original composition that captures the same doctrinal substance as both sources — the angel as God-given companion, protector from danger and temptation, carrier of prayers, and defender at the hour of death — and shares the same nine-day window. Its day-by-day arc (companion, wonder, protection, temptation, light, loneliness, prayers, death, vision of God) is close in spirit to PrayMore's role-based scheme, and its closing prayer echoes the traditional "enlighten, guard, rule, and guide." The wording is Ember's own rather than a quotation of either source.
