# Any feast of Our Lady (generic) — fidelity comparison

## Ember (this app) — en-US
**Opening prayer:** O Mary, honored [under this title/feast], I come before you as a child before its mother. You who are full of grace and never turn away from those who run to you, look upon me with the same love with which you watched over your Son. Lead me by your hand to Jesus, and obtain for me the graces I most need. Amen.

**Day 1 — Mary, full of grace:** The angel greeted Mary as one already filled with God's favor, untouched by sin from her first moment. In her the Lord prepared a dwelling worthy of His Son, and her holiness is the firstfruit of the redemption He won for us all.  _(Intention: For the grace to be purified of sin and made a fitting dwelling for God.)_

**Day 2 — Her humble fiat:** When God asked everything of her, Mary answered, "Let it be done to me according to your word." In that quiet surrender the whole of salvation turned, and she teaches us that true greatness is found in saying yes to God.  _(Intention: For the courage to say yes to God even when His will is hard.)_

**Day 3 — Mother of God:** Mary bore in her womb the eternal Word and held God Himself in her arms. Because she is Mother of the Head, she is Mother of all His members, and so she is truly our Mother too.  _(Intention: For a deeper trust in Mary as my true and loving Mother.)_

**Day 4 — Her faithful love:** From Bethlehem to Calvary, Mary followed her Son with a love that never wavered, even when a sword pierced her own soul. She shows us that fidelity is proven not in comfort but in standing firm beneath the cross.  _(Intention: For perseverance in faith during trials and sorrows.)_

**Day 5 — My particular intention:** Mary kept all things, pondering them in her heart, and she knows the weight of what each of us carries. Today I place before her the particular need that brought me to this novena, confident that she will carry it to her Son.  _(Intention: For the particular intention I now entrust to Our Lady's hands.)_

**Day 6 — Mediatrix of graces:** At Cana, Mary noticed the need before anyone asked and quietly brought it to Jesus, who acted at her word. So she does for us still, presenting our prayers to her Son and obtaining what we could never win alone.  _(Intention: For confidence that Mary brings my needs to Jesus and is heard.)_

**Day 7 — Refuge of sinners:** No one who has fled to Mary's protection has ever been left unaided. She does not shrink from our wounds but draws near, gently turning the lost heart back toward the mercy of God.  _(Intention: For the conversion of sinners and the return of those far from God.)_

**Day 8 — Queen of heaven:** Taken body and soul into glory, Mary reigns beside her Son as Queen and Mother of the redeemed. Her crown is not distant majesty but a mother's authority bent toward the good of her children.  _(Intention: For the grace to live now as a citizen of the heaven Mary already shares.)_

**Day 9 — Entrusted to her heart:** Beneath the cross Jesus gave us to Mary and Mary to us, that we might find a home in her heart. On this final day I consecrate myself wholly to her, asking her to keep and guide me all the days of my life.  _(Intention: For the grace to live ever consecrated to the Immaculate Heart of Mary.)_

(After every day: one Our Father, one Hail Mary, one Glory Be.)

**Closing prayer:** Most holy Virgin Mary, I thank you for hearing the prayer of these nine days. Present my petitions to your Son, and if what I ask is for the good of my soul, obtain it for me; and if it is not, grant me instead the grace to want only what God wills. Keep me always near to your maternal heart. Amen.
