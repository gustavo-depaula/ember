Hearth

Static content for the Ember Catholic prayer app. The hearth sustains the ember.

  • manifest.json — file inventory with hashes
  • v1/bible/drb/ — Douay-Rheims Bible (73 books)
  • v1/propers/ — EF Mass propers (tempora + sancti)
  • v1/catechism/ — Catechism of the Catholic Church
  • v1/saints/ — Saint images (PNG + WebP)
