***Verbum supernum***

The celestial word coming forth,but not leaving the right hand of the Father,going out to his work,comes to the evening of life.

- Handed over by a discipleto his enemies for his death,he first gave himself to his disciplesin the food of life.
- He gave them his flesh and bloodunder two species,so that he might feed the whole manof twofold substance.
- Being born he gave himself as a companion, dining he gave himself as nourishment,dying he gave himself as a ransom,reigning he gives himself as a reward.
- O saving Victim,who opens the door of heaven,wars and enemies press us,give us strength, bring us help.
- To the Lord, one and three,be eternal praise,may he give us eternal lifein the homeland. Amen.
