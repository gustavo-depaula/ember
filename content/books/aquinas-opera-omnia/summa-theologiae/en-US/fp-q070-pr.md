# Question 70 — The Work of Adornment, and of the Fourth Day

We must next consider the work of adornment, first as to each day by itself, second as to all seven days in general.  

In the first place, then, we consider the work of the fourth day, second, that of the fifth day, third, that of the sixth day, and fourth, such matters as belong to the seventh day.  

Under the first head there are three points of inquiry:  

(1) As to the production of the lights;  

- (2) As to the end of their production;
- (3) Whether they are living beings?
