# Question 75 — The Resurrection

In the next place we must consider things connected with and accompanying the resurrection: (1) The resurrection itself; (2) The cause of the resurrection; (3) Its time and manner; (4) Its starting terminus; (5) The condition of those who rise again.  

Under the first head there will be three points of inquiry:  

(1) Whether there is to be a resurrection of the body?  

- (2) Whether it is universally of all bodies?
- (3) Whether it is natural or miraculous?
