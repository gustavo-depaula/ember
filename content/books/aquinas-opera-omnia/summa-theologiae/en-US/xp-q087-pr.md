# Question 87 — Men’s Knowledge of Merits and Demerits at the Judgment

In the next place we must treat of those things which follow the resurrection. (1) The knowledge which, after rising again, men will have at the judgment concerning merits and demerits; (2) The general judgment itself, as also the time and place at which it will be; (3) Who will judge and who will be judged; (4) The form wherein the judge will come to judge; (5) What will be after the judgment, the state of the world and of those who will have risen again.  

Under the first head there are three points of inquiry:  

(1) Whether at the judgment every man will know all his sins?  

- (2) Whether every one will be able to read all that is on another’s conscience?
- (3) Whether one will be able at one glance to see all merits and demerits?
