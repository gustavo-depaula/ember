# Question 15 — The Means of Making Satisfaction

***We must now consider the means of making satisfaction.***

Under this head there are three points of inquiry:  

(1) Whether satisfaction must be made by means of penal works?  

- (2) Whether the scourges by which God punishes man in this life are satisfactory?
- (3) Whether the works of satisfaction are suitably reckoned by saying that there are three, namely, almsgiving, fasting, and prayer?
