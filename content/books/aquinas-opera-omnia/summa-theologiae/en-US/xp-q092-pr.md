# Question 92 — The Vision of the Divine Essence in Reference to the Blessed

In the next place we must consider matters concerning the blessed after the general judgment. We shall consider: (1) Their vision of the divine essence, wherein their bliss chiefly consists; (2) Their bliss and their mansions; (3) Their relations with the damned; (4) Their gifts, which are contained in their bliss; (5) The crowns which perfect and adorn their happiness.  

Under the first head there are three points of inquiry:  

(1) Whether the saints will see God in his essence?  

- (2) Whether they will see him with the eyes of the body?
- (3) Whether, in seeing God, they will see all that God sees?
