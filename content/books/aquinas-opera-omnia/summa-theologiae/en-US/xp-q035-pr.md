# Question 35 — The Effect of Holy Orders

***We must next consider the effect of this sacrament.***

Under this head there are five points of inquiry:  

(1) Whether sanctifying grace is conferred in the sacrament of holy orders?  

- (2) Whether a character is imprinted in connection with all the orders?
- (3) Whether the character of holy orders presupposes of necessity the character of baptism?
- (4) Whether it presupposes of necessity the character of confirmation?
- (5) Whether the character of one order presupposes of necessity the character of another order?
