# To You, O God, Fountain of Mercy

***To you, O God,Fountain of MercyI come as a sinnerthat you would deign to wash awaymy uncleanness.***

> O Sun of Justice,give sight to a blind man.O Eternal Healer,cure one who is wounded.O King of Kings,clothe the destitute.O Mediator between God and man,restore the reprobate.O Good Shepherd,lead back the stray.

> Give, O God,

> mercy to the wretched,reprieve to the criminal,life to the dead,justification to the sinful,and, to the hard of heart,the anointing of grace.

> O Most Merciful,

> call back those who flee,draw back those who resist,raise up those who fall,watch over those who stand,lead forward those who stray.

> Do not forget the one who forgets you.Do not forsake the one who forsakes you.Do not despise the sinner.

> For I, by sinning,have offended you, my God.I have hurt my neighbor,and have not spared myself.

> O God, Almighty Father,in weaknessI have sinned against you.

> O God, all-knowing Son,in ignoranceI have sinned against you.

> O God, Gentle Holy Spirit,in maliceI have sinned against you.

> In all of these things I have offended you,most high Trinity.Woe to me, a pitiful soul!How many,how great, and how diversethe faultsI have committed!I have abandoned you, Lord.

> I have opposed your goodness,surrenderingto a harmful love, andby a harmful fearI am brought low,preferring thusto lose youthan be bereft of what I loved,to offend youthan avoidthe things I should have feared.

> O my God,how much harm I have doneby word and by deed,by sinningsecretly, openly, and defiantly!

> Thus, out of my weakness,I beseech younot to pay heedto my iniquitybut to your immense goodness,and mercifullyto pardon what I have done,granting me sorrowfor what is in the pastand an efficacious warningfor the future.Amen.

> I Praise, Glorify, and Bless You, My God
