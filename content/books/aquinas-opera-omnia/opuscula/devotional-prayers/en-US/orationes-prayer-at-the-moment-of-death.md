# Prayer at the Moment of Death

***I receive you,price of the redemption of my soul;I receive you,viaticum of my journey;for love of whomI have studied,I have kept watch,and I have worked.***

> You have I preached; you have I taught.I have never said anything against you,but if I have said anything,I have said it in ignorance,nor am I obstinate in my judgment.

> And if I have said anything badly,about this sacrament or anything else,I leave everything to the correctionof the holy Roman Church,in whose obedienceI now take leave of this life.Amen.
