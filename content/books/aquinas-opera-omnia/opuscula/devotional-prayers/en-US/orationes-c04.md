# Sweetest Jesus

***Sweetest Jesus,***
***Body and Blood most holy,***
***be the delight***
***and pleasure of my soul,***
***my salvation***
***and strength in all temptation,***
***my joy***
***and peace in every trial,***
***my light***
***and support in every word and deed, my final protection***
***in death.***
***Amen.***
