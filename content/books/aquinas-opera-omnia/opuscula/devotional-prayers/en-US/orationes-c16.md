# Prayer at the Moment of Death

Prayer of Thomas Aquinas on his deathbed after receiving the Blessed Sacrament *(See *Ystoria sancti Thome de Aquino de Guillaume de Tocco*, ch. 58, 198, ll. 50–55. Translation by The Aquinas Institute.)*  

***I receive you,***
***price of the redemption of my soul;***
***I receive you,***
***viaticum of my journey;***
***for love of whom***
***I have studied,***
***I have kept watch,***
***and I have worked.***

> You have I preached; you have I taught.
> I have never said anything against you,
> but if I have said anything,
> I have said it in ignorance,
> nor am I obstinate in my judgment.

> And if I have said anything badly,
> about this sacrament or anything else,
> I leave everything to the correction
> of the holy Roman Church,
> in whose obedience
> I now take leave of this life.
> Amen.
