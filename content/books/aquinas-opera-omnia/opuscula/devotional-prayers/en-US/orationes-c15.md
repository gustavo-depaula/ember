# My Dear Jesus

Prayer for the preservation of chastity *(The authenticity of this prayer is doubtful. The Italian of the prayer can be found in “Analecta sacri ordinis fratrum praedicatorum,” vol. 16 (January-February, 1923), 365. The English translation is taken from the *St. Thomas Manual*, 91.)*  

***My dear Jesus,***
***I well know that every perfect gift,***
***and above all others, the gift of chastity***
***depends upon the powerful influence of your grace,***
***and that without you***
***no creature can do anything.***

> I ask you, therefore, to protect with your grace
> the chastity and purity of my soul and body.

> And should I experience in myself a sensual impression,
> that could sully chastity and purity,
> banish it from me,
> O you who are the supreme Lord
> of all the powers of my soul,
> that with a spotless heart I may walk in your love and service,
> whilst every day of my life I sacrifice myself pure and chaste upon the most pure altar of your Divinity!
> Amen.
