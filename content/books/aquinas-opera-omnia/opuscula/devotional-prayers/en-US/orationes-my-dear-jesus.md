# My Dear Jesus

***My dear Jesus,I well know that every perfect gift,and above all others, the gift of chastitydepends upon the powerful influence of your grace,and that without youno creature can do anything.***

> I ask you, therefore, to protect with your gracethe chastity and purity of my soul and body.

> And should I experience in myself a sensual impression,that could sully chastity and purity,banish it from me,O you who are the supreme Lordof all the powers of my soul,that with a spotless heart I may walk in your love and service,whilst every day of my life I sacrifice myself pure and chaste upon the most pure altar of your Divinity!Amen.

> Prayer at the Moment of Death
