# To You, O God, Fountain of Mercy

***To you, O God,***
***Fountain of Mercy***
***I come as a sinner***
***that you would deign to wash away***
***my uncleanness.***

> O Sun of Justice,
> give sight to a blind man.
> O Eternal Healer,
> cure one who is wounded.
> O King of Kings,
> clothe the destitute.
> O Mediator between God and man,
> restore the reprobate.
> O Good Shepherd,
> lead back the stray.

> Give, O God,

> mercy to the wretched,
> reprieve to the criminal,
> life to the dead,
> justification to the sinful,
> and, to the hard of heart,
> the anointing of grace.

> O Most Merciful,

> call back those who flee,
> draw back those who resist,
> raise up those who fall,
> watch over those who stand,
> lead forward those who stray.

> Do not forget the one who forgets you.
> Do not forsake the one who forsakes you.
> Do not despise the sinner.

> For I, by sinning,
> have offended you, my God.
> I have hurt my neighbor,
> and have not spared myself.

> O God, Almighty Father,
> in weakness
> I have sinned against you.

> O God, all-knowing Son,
> in ignorance
> I have sinned against you.

> O God, Gentle Holy Spirit,
> in malice
> I have sinned against you.

> In all of these things I have offended you,
> most high Trinity.
> Woe to me, a pitiful soul!
> How many,
> how great, and how diverse
> the faults
> I have committed!
> I have abandoned you, Lord.

> I have opposed your goodness,
> surrendering
> to a harmful love, and
> by a harmful fear
> I am brought low,
> preferring thus
> to lose you
> than be bereft of what I loved,
> to offend you
> than avoid
> the things I should have feared.

> O my God,
> how much harm I have done
> by word and by deed,
> by sinning
> secretly, openly, and defiantly!

> Thus, out of my weakness,
> I beseech you
> not to pay heed
> to my iniquity
> but to your immense goodness,
> and mercifully
> to pardon what I have done,
> granting me sorrow
> for what is in the past
> and an efficacious warning
> for the future.
> Amen.
