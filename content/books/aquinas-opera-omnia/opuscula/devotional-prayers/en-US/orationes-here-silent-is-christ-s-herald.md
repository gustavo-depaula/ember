# Here silent is Christ’s Herald

***Here silent is Christ’s Herald;here quenched, the people’s light;here lies the martyred championwho fought Faith’s holy fight.***

> The voice the sheep heard gladly,the light they loved to see;he fell beneath the weaponsof graceless Cathari.

> The Savior crowns his soldier;his praise the people psalm.The Faith he kept adorns himwith martyr’s fadeless palm.

> His praise new marvels utter,new light he spreads abroadand now the whole wide cityknows well the path to God.

> The Angelic Cross
