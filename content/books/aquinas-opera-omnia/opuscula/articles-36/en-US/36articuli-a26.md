# Article 26

The Commentator [Averroes] puts forward this explanation while commenting on these words from the seventh book of the *Metaphysics*: *therefore as it is said that in substances, the principle is*. He says, *for that reason, Aristotle says in* De Animalibus *that the powers that are in the semen are like the intellect because they act by the activity of the intellect and powers do not act by a bodily instrument*. Averroes on VII *Metaph.* comm. 31 (ed. Venetiis 1552, vol. VIII, fol. 85 va 18-21).
