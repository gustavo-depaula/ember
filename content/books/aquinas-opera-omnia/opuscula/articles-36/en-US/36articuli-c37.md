# Epilogue

These things, most dear brother, that I say in response to the articles sent by you are more expansive than you have asked because it was not possible to perfectly respond to those things that were able to have a variety of meanings. This is especially so since you did not write objections that were put forward against articles of this sort. If you had, it would have been possible to more perfectly and certainly respond. May your charity thrive yet more and may you compensate me for this labor by intercessory prayer.
