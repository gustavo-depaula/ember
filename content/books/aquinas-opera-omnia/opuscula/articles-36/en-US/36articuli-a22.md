# Article 22

I do not see why this would not be permitted—unless, perhaps, raising the question would cause scandal for some people. For it does sometimes happen that simple souls hear that even those things that are of the Faith are disputed in places of learning and they think they are disputed because the truth of the Faith is itself called into question. However, in order to eliminate such a false understanding, someone who debates the issue could testify that he does not call the truth of the matter into doubt and that he merely raises the question for the sake of examining the foundation of the truth.
