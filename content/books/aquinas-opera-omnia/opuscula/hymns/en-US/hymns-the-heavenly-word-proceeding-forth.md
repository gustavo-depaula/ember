# The Heavenly Word Proceeding Forth

***The heavenly Word proceeding forth,yet leaving not the Father’s side,and going to his work on earth,had reached at length life’s eventide.***

- By false disciple to be givento foemen for his life athirst,himself, the living bread from heaven,he gave to his disciples first.
- In twofold form of sacrament,he gave his flesh, he gave his blood,that man, of soul and body blent,might wholly feed on mystic food.
- By birth man’s fellow-man was he,his meat while sitting at the board;he died, our ransomer to be,he reigns to be our great reward.
- O saving Victim, opening widethe gate of heaven to man below;our foes press hard on every side,thine aid supply, thy strength bestow.
- All praise and thanks to thee ascendfor evermore, blest one in three;O grant us life that shall not end,in our true native land with thee. Amen.
Praise, O Zion
