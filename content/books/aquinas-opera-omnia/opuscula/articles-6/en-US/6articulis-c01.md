# Prologue

To one most dear to him in Christ, Brother Gerard of Besançon, Brother Thomas of Aquino of the same order sends greetings with fraternal love. I have received your letter containing certain articles about which you have asked me to write back and, although I was occupied with many things, lest I be lacking in regard to charitable petition, I have taken care to respond to you as soon as the opportunity to do so presented itself.
