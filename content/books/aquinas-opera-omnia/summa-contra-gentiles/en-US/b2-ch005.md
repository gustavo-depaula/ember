# Chapter 5 — Order of the things to be said

We shall treat of these things in the following order. First, we shall discourse of the bringing forth of things into being; *(Ch. 6.)* second, of their distinction; *(Ch. 39.)* third, of the nature of these same things brought forth and distinct from one another, so far as it concerns the truth of faith. *(Ch. 46.)*
