# Lecture 18

^4:18^ Sade. Our steps have slipped in the way of our streets, our end draws near: our days are fulfilled, for our end is come.  

> **84**. Second, he excludes help from their own forces: ***our steps have slipped***, as if from weakness we could not stand firmly, and as if the roads were dark and slippery. *Let their way become dark and slippery; and let the angel of the Lord pursue them* (Ps 34:6)*. Their way shall be as a slippery way in the dark* (Jer 23:12). And therefore, ***our end draws near***, namely, our captivity, or death; and finally, ***our days are fulfilled***. *The end is come, the end is come* (Ezek 7:2).
