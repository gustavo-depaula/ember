# Lecture 15

^4:15^ Samech. Depart you that are defiled, they cried out to them: depart, get ye hence, touch not: for they quarreled, and being removed, they said among the gentiles: he will no more dwell among them.  

> **81**. Here he presents the mockery of their enemies against the captive citizens.

And first, he reproaches them for the profanation of the holy places: ***they cried out***, namely, their enemies, ***to them***, namely, the Jews; ***depart***, from the holy place into captivity; ***touch not***, the sacred things, you impure. *Whoever of the seed of Aaron the priest has a blemish, he shall not approach to offer sacrifices to the Lord* (Lev 21:21).  

Second, he reproaches them for God’s indignation, in regard to the deprivation of divine help: ***they quarreled***, their enemies against the Jews; ***and being removed***, on both sides, some by quarreling, others by sorrowing; ***he will no more dwell***, God defending you. *Saying: God has forsaken him* (Ps 70:11).
