# Lecture 15

^2:15^ Samech. All they that passed by the way have clapped their hands at you: they have hissed and wagged their heads at the daughter of Jerusalem, saying, ‘Is this the city of perfect beauty, the joy of all the earth?’  

> **37**. Here he presents the astonishment of the spectators.

And first, of friends;  

second, of enemies, at ***all your enemies have opened their mouth against you*** (Lam 2:16).  

Concerning the first, he presents the astonishment of compassionate friends, which is expressed by four signs. By the clapping of hands: ***all they that passed by the way***, of your land, seeing the devastation of the land, ***have clapped their hands***. By hissing: ***they have hissed***. By shaking their heads: ***and wagged their heads***. By words: ***saying, ‘Is this the city of perfect beauty,’*** because in things spiritual and temporal (for it was a royal and priestly city), it was ***‘the joy of all the earth,’*** for they came to her from everywhere for the solemn feasts. *With the joy of the whole earth is mount Zion founded, on the sides of the north, the city of the great king* (Ps 47:3). *A populous city, a joyous city* (Isa 22:2). *And many nations shall pass by this city: and they shall say every man to his neighbor: why has the Lord done so to this great city?* (Jer 22:8).
