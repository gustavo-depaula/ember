# Lecture 17

^2:17^ Ain. The Lord has done that which he purposed, he has fulfilled his word, which he commanded in the days of old: he has destroyed, and has not spared, and he has caused the enemy to rejoice over you, and has set up the horn of your adversaries.  

> **39**. Here he presents the approval of the judge.

And first, there is his consistency in doing what he proposed: ***the Lord has done that which he purposed***. *This is the counsel, that I have purposed* (Isa 14:26).  

Second, there is shown the veracity of his speech, ***he has fulfilled his word***: for he decreed the destruction of the people ***in the days of old*** (Deut 28:20). Or also by previous prophets. *Has he said then, and will he not do? Has he spoken, and will he not fulfill?* (Num 23:19).  

Third, there is shown what is just in the action: ***he has destroyed***, Judea; ***and has not spared***, as if abandoning the rigor of justice; ***the horn***, that is, power. *You have set up the right hand of them that oppress him* (Ps 88:43).
