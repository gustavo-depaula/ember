# Lecture 12

^4:12^ Lamed. The kings of the earth, and all the inhabitants of the world would not have believed, that the adversary and the enemy should enter in by the gates of Jerusalem.  

> **78**. Here he shows that the event was astonishing because previously it would have been incredible; ***kings and inhabitants would not have believed***, when powerful kings and ramparts with divine protection were in the city; ***the adversary***, openly violent; ***and the enemy***, deceitfully hidden. *Who has taken this counsel against Tyre, that was formerly crowned?* (Isa 23:8)*. The destruction thereof shall come on a sudden, when it is not looked for* (Isa 30:13).
