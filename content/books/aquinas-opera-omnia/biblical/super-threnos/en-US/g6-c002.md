# Lecture 2

^4:2^ Beth. The noble sons of Zion, and they that were clothed with the first gold: how are they esteemed as earthen vessels, the work of the potter’s hands?  

> **68**. Here he explains what he said above: ***noble***, because from a holy race; ***with the first***, that is, best, ***gold***, namely, wisdom. *All gold in comparison of her is as a little sand* (Wis 7:9). ***As earthen vessels***, that is, a weak and lowly people. *And now, O Lord, you are our father, and we are clay* (Isa 64:8).
