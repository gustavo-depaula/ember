# Lecture 17

^4:17^ Ain. While we were yet standing, our eyes failed, expecting help for us in vain, when we looked attentively towards a nation that was not able to save us.  

> **83**. Here he excludes the possibility of escape, excluding four ways of possible escape.

First, the help of friends: ***while we were yet standing***, we were waiting, ***expecting help for us in vain***, namely, the Egyptians. *For Egypt shall help in vain, and to no purpose* (Isa 30:7).
