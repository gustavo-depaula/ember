# Lecture 19

^4:19^ Coph. Our persecutors were swifter than the eagles of the air: they pursued us upon the mountains, they lay in wait for us in the wilderness.  

> **85**. Third, he excludes the remedy of flight. Here he touches upon the history that is told in Jeremiah 52, when the Chaldeans surrounded Zedekiah fleeing upon a solitary road. Note the hyperbolic expression: *they shall fly as an eagle* (Hab 1:8).
