# Lecture 7

^2:7^ Zain. The Lord has cast off his altar, he has cursed his sanctuary: he has delivered the walls of the towers thereof into the hand of the enemy: they have made a noise in the house of the Lord, as in the day of a solemn feast.  

> **29**. Here he touches on the destruction of the structures that were in the temple.

And first, of the altar for worship: ***his altar***, of holocausts, ***the Lord has cast off***, handing it over to their enemies to be profaned; ***his sanctuary***, the sacrifices of propitiation that were offered there. *They saw the sanctuary desolate, and the altar profaned* (1 Macc 4:38). *The holy places are come into the hands of strangers* (1 Macc 2:8).  

Third, the burning of the temple by men: ***they have made a noise***, of blasphemies or cries of war; ***as in the day of a solemn feast***, as the priests were accustomed to use in praising God. *A voice of the people from the city, a voice from the temple, the voice of the Lord that renders recompense to his enemies* (Isa 66:6).
