# Lecture 10

^4:10^ Jod. The hands of the pitiful women have boiled their own children: they were their meat in the destruction of the daughter of my people.  

> **76**. Here he presents the immense hunger of mothers, who, in the excess of their hunger, ate their own children. Above: ***shall women then eat their own fruit, their children?*** (Lam 2:20).
