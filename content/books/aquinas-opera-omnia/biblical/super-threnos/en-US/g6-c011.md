# Lecture 11

^4:11^ Caph. The Lord has accomplished his wrath, he has poured out his fierce anger: and he has kindled a fire in Zion, and it has devoured the foundations thereof.  

> **77**. Here he weeps over the captivity and destruction of the city.

And first, he presents the destruction;  

second, the astonishment at this event, at ***the kings of the earth would not have believed*** (Lam 4:12);  

third, he gives the reason for the destruction, at ***for the sins of her prophets*** (Lam 4:13).  

***The Lord has accomplished***, bringing punishment; ***he has poured out***, on account of its abundance; ***he has kindled a fire***, on account of the kind of punishment, because the city was burned; ***the foundations***—either this is a chance example, or it is said because the houses were destroyed even to their foundations. *A fire is kindled in my wrath, and shall burn even to the lowest hell* (Deut 32:22).
