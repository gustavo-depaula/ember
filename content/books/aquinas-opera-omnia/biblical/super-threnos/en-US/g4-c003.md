# Lecture 3

^2:3^ Ghimel. He has broken in his fierce anger all the horn of Israel: he has drawn back his right hand from before the enemy: and he has kindled in Jacob, as it were, a flaming fire devouring round about.  

> **25**. Here he shows the impossibility of resisting.

And first, he excludes human strength: ***the horn***, that is, power. *And I will break all the horns of sinners* (Ps 74:11).  

Second, he excludes divine help: ***he has drawn back***, as it were, from the defense by which he had previously defended them. *Why do you turn away your hand even your right hand?* (Ps 73:11).  

Third, he concludes by showing the burning of the earth: ***and he has kindled***. *He set him on fire, and he understood not* (Isa 42:25).
