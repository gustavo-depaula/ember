# Prologue — Prologue

***1. Whether a spiritual substance is composed of matter and form?***

- 2. Whether a spiritual substance be united to a body?
- 3. Whether the spiritual substance, which is the human soul, is united to the body through a medium?
- 4. Whether the whole soul is in every part of the body?
- 5. Whether there is any created spiritual substance that is not united to a body?
- 6. Whether a spiritual substance is united to a heavenly body?
- 7. Whether a spiritual substance is united to an ethereal body?
- 8. Whether all angels differ in species from one another?
- 9. Whether the potential intellect is one in all men?
- 10. Whether the agent intellect is one intellect belonging to all men?
- 11. Whether the powers of the soul are the same as the essence of the soul?
