# Prologue — On the power of God

***1. Whether there is power in God?***

- 2. Whether God’s power is infinite?
- 3. Whether those things that are impossible for nature are possible for God?
- 4. Whether we ought to judge a thing’s possibility or impossibility in reference to its lower or its higher causes?
- 5. Whether God can do what he does not do and can leave undone what he does?
- 6. Whether God can do things that are possible to others, as, for example, to walk or to sin, etc.?
- 7. Whether God is called omnipotent?
