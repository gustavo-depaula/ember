# Prologue — On the simplicity of the divine essence

***1. Whether God is simple?***

- 2. Whether God’s essence or substance is the same as his existence?
- 3. Whether God is contained in a genus?
- 4. Whether good, wise, just, and the like predicate an accident in God?
- 5. Whether these terms signify the divine substance?
- 6. Whether these terms are synonymous?
- 7. Whether these terms are said of God and creatures univocally or equivocally?
- 8. Whether there is any relation between God and the creature?
- 9. Whether these relations between creatures and God are really in the creatures themselves?
- 10. Whether God is really related to the creature such that this relation is something in God?
- 11. Whether these temporal relations are in God according to account or reason?
