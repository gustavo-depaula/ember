# Prologue — On those things that are said of God relatively from eternity

1. Whether the relations said of God from eternity that are indicated by the names “Father” and “Son” are real or only relations of account or reason?  

- 2. Whether relation in God is his substance?
- 3. Whether the relations constitute and distinguish the persons and hypostases?
- 4. Whether, if relation is removed by the intellect, the hypostasis remains in the divine?
