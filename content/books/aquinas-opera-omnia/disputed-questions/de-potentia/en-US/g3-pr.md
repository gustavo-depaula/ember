# Prologue — On creation, the first effect of the divine power

***1. Whether God can create something?***

- 2. Whether creation is a change?
- 3. Whether creation is something real in the creature?
- 4. Whether the creative power is communicable to a creature?
- 5. Whether there can be anything that is not created by God?
- 6. Whether there is but one principle of creation?
- 7. Whether God works within every activity of nature?
- 8. Whether creation is blended with the work of nature?
- 9. Whether the soul is created?
- 10. Whether the soul is created in the body or apart from the body?
- 11. Whether the sensitive and vegetative souls exist through creation?
- 12. Whether they are in the seed when it is separated?
- 13. Whether any being that is from another can be eternal?
- 14. Whether it is possible for that which differs essentially from God to have existed always?
- 15. Whether things proceeded from God through a natural necessity?
- 16. Whether many things can proceed from one first thing?
- 17. Whether the world has always existed?
- 18. Whether the angels were created before the visible world?
- 19. Whether it would be possible for the angels to exist before the visible world?
