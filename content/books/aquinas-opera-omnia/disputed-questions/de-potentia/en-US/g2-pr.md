# Prologue — On the generative power in God

***1. Whether within the divine there is the power to beget?***

- 2. Whether the generative power in God is spoken of essentially or notionally?
- 3. Whether the generative power goes into the act of generation at the command of the will?
- 4. Whether there can be several sons in God?
- 5. Whether the generative power is included in omnipotence?
- 6. Whether the power to beget is the same as the power to create?
