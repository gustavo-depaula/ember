# Prologue — On the divine persons

***1. How are the persons compared with the essence, subsistence, and hypostasis?***

- 2. What is a “person”?
- 3. Whether there can be a person in God?
- 4. Whether this term “person” signifies something relative or something absolute in God?
- 5. Whether there is a number of persons in God?
- 6. Whether, when we speak of God, we may rightly use the word “person” in the plural?
- 7. How are numerical terms predicated of the divine persons, whether positively or only negatively?
- 8. Whether there is any diversity in God?
- 9. Whether in God there are only three persons, or more or fewer than three?
