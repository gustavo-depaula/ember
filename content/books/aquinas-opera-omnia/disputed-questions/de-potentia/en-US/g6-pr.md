# Prologue — On miracles

1. Whether God can do anything in creatures that is beyond nature, against nature, or contrary to the course of nature?  

- 2. Whether everything that God does without natural causes or contrary to the course of nature can be called a miracle?
- 3. Whether spiritual creatures can work miracles by their natural power?
- 4. Whether good angels and men can work miracles by some gift of grace?
- 5. Whether the demons also cooperate in the working of miracles?
- 6. Whether angels and demons have bodies naturally united to them?
- 7. Whether angels or demons can assume bodies?
- 8. Whether an angel or demon by means of an assumed body can exercise the functions of a living body?
- 9. Whether the working of a miracle should be attributed to faith?
- 10. Whether by means of certain sensible and corporeal objects, deeds, or words, the demons are forced to work the miracles that seem to occur by magic?
