# Prooemium

***De creatione materiae informis***

Primo quaeritur utrum creatio materiae informis praecesserit duratione creationem rerum.  

- Secundo utrum materiae formatio *(The Marietti reads *materia informis* (“unformed matter”), but we have altered it as *materiae formatio* (“the formation of matter”), both for greater intelligibility and to conform it to the second article itself.)* tota simul fuerit, an successive.
