# Prologue — On the knowledge of Christ

***1. Whether we say that there is created knowledge in Christ?***

- 2. Whether the soul of Christ sees the Word through any habit?
- 3. Whether Christ had other knowledge of things than that by which he knows them in the Word?
- 4. Whether the soul of Christ knows in the Word all that the Word knows?
- 5. Whether the soul of Christ knows all that God could make?
- 6. Whether the soul of Christ knows everything with that knowledge by which it knows things in their proper nature?
