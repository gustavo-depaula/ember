# Prologue — On the will of God

***1. Whether it belongs to God to have a will?***

- 2. Whether the divine will can be distinguished by antecedent and consequent?
- 3. Whether God's will is suitably divided by the will of good pleasure and the will of sign?
- 4. Whether God wills of necessity everything that he wills?
- 5. Whether the divine will imposes necessity upon the things willed?
- 6. Whether justice in created things things depends upon simple divine will?
- 7. Whether we are bound to conform our will to the divine will?
- 8. Whether we are bound to conform our will to the divine will in what is willed, namely, whether we are bound to will what we know that God wills?
