# Prologue — On the grace of Christ

***1. Whether there is created grace in Christ?***

- 2. Whether habitual grace is required in order for human nature to be united to the Word in the person?
- 3. Whether Christ's grace is infinite?
- 4. Whether the grace of headship belongs to Christ according to human nature?
- 5. Whether habitual grace is required to be in Christ in order for him to be head?
- 6. Whether Christ was able to merit?
- 7. Whether Christ was able to merit for others?
- 8. Whether Christ was able to merit in the first moment of his conception?
