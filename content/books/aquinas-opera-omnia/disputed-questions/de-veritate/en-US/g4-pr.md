# Prologue — On the Word

***1. Whether there can be a word, properly speaking, in God?***

- 2. Whether "Word" is predicated of God essentially or only personally?
- 3. Whether "Word" is a proper name of the Holy Spirit?
- 4. Whether the Father utters all creatures in the "Word" by which he utters himself?
- 5. Whether the "Word" implies a relation to creatures?
- 6. Whether things exist more truly in the "Word" or in themselves?
- 7. Whether the "Word" is related to those things which do not exist, will not exist, and never have existed?
- 8. Whether all created things are life within the "Word?"
