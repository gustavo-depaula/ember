# Prologue — On the passions of the soul

***1. Whether the soul naturally undergoes anything from the body?***

- 2. How the soul suffers when conjoined to the body?
- 3. Whether passion is only in the sensitive appetite?
- 4. In what respect there is contrariety and diversity between the soul's passions?
- 5. Whether hope, fear, joy, and sadness are the principal passions?
- 6. Whether we merit by the passions?
- 7. Whether a passion joined to merit takes something away from merit?
- 8. Whether there were passions such as these in Christ?
- 9. Whether there was in Christ's soul the passion of pain with respect to his higher reason?
- 10. Whether the pleasure of enjoyment in Christ's higher reason was impeded by the pain of the Passion, and conversely?
