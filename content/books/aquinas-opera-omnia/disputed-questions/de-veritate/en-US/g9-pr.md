# Prologue — On the communication of angelic knowledge

***1. Whether one angel illuminates another?***

- 2. Whether an inferior angel is always illumined by a superior angel or is he sometimes illumined directly by God?
- 3. Whether one angel cleanses another when he illumines him?
- 4. Whether one angel speaks to another?
- 5. Whether the inferior angels speak to the superior?
- 6. Whether there is a determinate local distance required in order for one angel to speak to another?
- 7. Whether one angel speaks to another in such a way that others will not know what he is saying?
