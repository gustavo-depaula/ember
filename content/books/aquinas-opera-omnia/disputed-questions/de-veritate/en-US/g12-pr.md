# Prologue — On prophecy

***1. Whether prophecy is a habit or an act?***

- 2. Whether prophecy deals with conclusions which can be known scientifically?
- 3. Whether prophecy is natural?
- 4. Whether some natural disposition is needed for prophecy?
- 5. Whether moral goodness is required for prophecy?
- 6. Whether the prophets see in the mirror of eternity?
- 7. Whether God in the revelation is made to a prophet imprint on the mind of the prophet new species of things or only intellectual light?
- 8. Whether all prophetic revelation takes place through the mediation of an angel?
- 9. Whether a prophet always loses sense-consciousness when he is under the influence of the spirit of prophecy?
- 10. Whether prophecy is suitably divided into prophecy of predestination, foreknowledge, and threats?
- 11. Whether there is unchangeable truth in prophecy?
- 12. Whether the prophecy according to the sight of understanding alone is higher than that which has the sight of understanding together with imagination?
- 13. Whether the grades of prophecy are distinguished according to the sight of imagination?
- 14. Whether Moses was more outstanding than other prophets?
