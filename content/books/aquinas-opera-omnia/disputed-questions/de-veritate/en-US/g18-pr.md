# Prologue — On the knowledge of the first man in the state of innocence

***1. Whether man in the state of innocence knew God through his essence?***

- 2. Whether man in the state of innocence saw God through creatures?
- 3. Whether Adam in the state of innocence had faith about God?
- 4. Whether Adam in the state of innocence had knowledge of all creatures?
- 5. Whether Adam in the state of innocence saw the angels through their essence?
- 6. Whether Adam in the state of innocence could be mistaken or deceived?
- 7. Whether the children who were born of Adam in the state of innocence had full knowledge of all things, as Adam did?
- 8. Whether in the state of innocence, children have the full use of reason immediately at birth?
