# Prologue — On the desire for the good

***1. Whether all things desire the good?***

- 2. Whether all things desire God himself?
- 3. Whether appetite is a special power of the soul?
- 4. Whether the will in rational things is a power different than the appetite of the sensitive part?
- 5. Whether the will wants anything from necessity?
- 6. Whether the will wants from necessity anything that it wants?
- 7. Whether someone merits by willing what one wants by necessity?
- 8. Whether God can coerce the will?
- 9. Whether any creature can change the will or make an impression upon it?
- 10. Whether the will and the intellect are the same power?
- 11. Whether the will is a higher power than the intellect, or conversely?
- 12. Whether the will moves the intellect and the other powers of the soul?
- 13. Whether intention is an act of the will?
- 14. Whether the will wishes the end and intends what is toward the end by the same motion?
- 15. Whether choice is an act of the will?
