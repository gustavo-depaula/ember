# Prologue — On sensuality

***1. Whether sensuality is a cognitive or appetitive power alone?***

- 2. Whether sensuality is one simple power, or else is divided into many powers, namely the irascible and the concupiscible?
- 3. Whether the irascible and the concupiscible are only in the lower appetite, or else in the higher appetite?
- 4. Whether sensuality obeys reason?
- 5. Whether there can be sin in sensuality?
- 6. Whether the concupiscible is more corrupt and infected than the irascible?
- 7. Whether sensuality can be cured in this life from the aforesaid corruption?
