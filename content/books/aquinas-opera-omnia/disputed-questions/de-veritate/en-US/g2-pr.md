# Prologue — On the knowledge of God

***1. Whether there is knowledge in God?***

- 2. Whether God knows or understands himself?
- 3. Whether God knows things other than himself?
- 4. Whether God has proper and determinate knowledge of things?
- 5. Whether God knows singular things?
- 6. Whether the human intellect knows singular?
- 7. Whether God knows the singular as now existing or not existing?
- 8. Whether God knows non-beings, and things that are not, have not been, and will not be?
- 9. Whether God knows infinite things?
- 10. Whether God can make infinite things?
- 11. Whether knowledge predicated of God and man is purely equivocal?
- 12. Whether God knows singular future contingents?
- 13. Whether God's knowledge changes?
- 14. Whether God's knowledge is the cause of things?
- 15. Whether God has knowledge of evil things?
