# Prologue — On the justification of the wicked

***1. Whether the justification of the wicked is the remission of sins?***

- 2. Whether the remission of sins can happen without grace?
- 3. Whether free choice is required for the justification of the wicked?
- 4. What motion of free choice is needed for justification, namely, whether a motion toward God is needed?
- 5. Whether a movement of free choice toward sin is required?
- 6. Whether the infusion of grace is the same as the remission of guilt?
- 7. Whether the remission of guilt naturally precedes the infusion of grace?
- 8. Whether the motion of free choice in the justification of the wicked naturally precedes the infusion of grace?
- 9. Whether the justification of the wicked happens in a moment?
