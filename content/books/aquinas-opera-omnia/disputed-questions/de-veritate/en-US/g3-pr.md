# Prologue — On ideas

***1. Whether there are ideas in God?***

- 2. Whether there are many ideas?
- 3. Whether ideas belong to speculative or only to practical knowledge?
- 4. Whether there is an idea of evil in God?
- 5. Whether there is an idea of first matter in God?
- 6. Whether there are ideas in God of those things that do not exist, will not exist, and have not existed?
- 7. Whether there are ideas of accidents in God?
- 8. Whether there are ideas of singulars in God?
