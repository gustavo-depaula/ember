# Prologue — On free choice

***1. Whether it belongs to man to have free choice?***

- 2. Whether there is free choice in brute animals?
- 3. Whether there is free choice in God?
- 4. Whether or not free choice is a power?
- 5. Whether free choice is one power or many?
- 6. Whether free choice is a will or a power other than the will?
- 7. Whether there could be some creature that naturally has a free choice confirmed in the good?
- 8. Whether the free choice of a creature could be confirmed in the good by a gift of grace?
- 9. Whether a man's free choice in this life's condition could be confirmed in the good?
- 10. Whether the free choice of any creature could be obstinate or unalterably fastened in evil?
- 11. Whether the free choice of a man in the state of this life could be obstinate in evil?
- 12. Whether free choice without grace in the sate of mortal sin can avoid mortal sin?
- 13. Whether someone in grace can avoid mortal sin?
- 14. Whether free choice without grace is capable of goodness?
- 15. Whether a man without grace could prepare himself for possessing grace?
