# Prologue — On providence

***1. To which attribute can God's providence be reduced?***

- 2. Whether the world is ruled by providence?
- 3. Whether God's providence extends to corruptible things?
- 4. Whether the motions and actions of all bodies here below are subject to divine providence?
- 5. Whether human acts are ruled by providence?
- 6. Whether brute animals and their acts are subject to God's providence?
- 7. Whether sinners are ruled by God's providence?
- 8. Whether all material creatures are governed by God's providence through angels?
- 9. Whether divine providence disposes bodies here below by means of the celestial bodies?
- 10. Whether human acts are governed by God's providence through the instrumentality of celestial bodies?
