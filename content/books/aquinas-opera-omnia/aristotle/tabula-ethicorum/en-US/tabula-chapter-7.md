# Chapter 7

***To Be Joyful***

***Proper upbringing is this: to be joyful in what is joyful; and sorrowful in what is sorrowful.***

Term 2  

***Grammarian***

***A man is not made a grammarian by uttering something correctly by chance or aided by someone.***

Term 3  

***Taste***

***Taste is judgment of flavors, what those who sample vine and season sauces do.***

Term 4  

***The Throat***

***A certain man prayed that his throat were longer than a crane's so that by having more contact with his food he might delight in it more.***

Term 5  

***Gratitude***

***It is proper to gratitude that one returns favour for favour, for one must be of service to one who benefits him.***

Term 6  

***Gnome***

***Man has *gnome*, *synesis*, and intellect by nature because they develop innately at a certain age.***

All men take pleasure in food, wine, and sex, but not as they ought.
