# Surge.S1.2

First: fear, mostly: *the lazy man says: there is a lion in the way* (Prov 26:13). The lazy man says this whenever he sees some difficulty, believing that he would fail at once if he were to endure that difficulty. There is *a lioness in the roads* (Prov 26:13), that is, he also says, “I am weak; I cannot endure it.”
