# Surge.S1.5

First, indeed, there is the love of his own external goods. *As the door turns upon its hinges*, and nevertheless is not moved by its hinges, *so does the lazy man turn upon his bed* (Prov 26:14), that is, in his riches, where he rests, in the way one rests in a bed. He is turned by the preachers, but he is not moved by them.
