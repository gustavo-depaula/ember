# EcceEgo.S1-1.7

So note, regarding all these things, that, just as the Savior was sent,Luke 1:26–38; cf. *ST* III.30. so also the precursor was sent to us by the Father through the commission of an angel, not through nature.John the Baptist: his coming was through a gracious act of God: Luke 1:10–20, 24–25; cf. *ST* III.27.1 and 6; cf. Gregory the Great, *Homilies on the Gospels*, homily 34, para. 8 (CCSL 141:306; PL 76:1250C); Aquinas, *In II Sent*. 9.1.4, ad 2. For they have truly been our angels who were sent to us in the name of God,
