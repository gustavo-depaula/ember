# AveMaria.S1.31

He, too, is revered by the angels: *benediction and glory and wisdom and thanksgiving, honor and power and strength, to our God* (Rev 7:12). And he is glorified by men: *every tongue should confess that the Lord Jesus Christ is in the glory of God the Father* (Phil 2:11); *blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord* (Ps 118 [117]:26).
