# AveMaria.S1.11

The first is that in regard to her soul she was full of grace. The grace of God is given for two chief purposes, namely to do good and to avoid evil. The blessed Virgin, then, received grace in the most perfect degree, because she had avoided every sin more than any other saint apart from Christ. For sin is either original, and from this she was cleansed in the womb,See *ST* III.27.3, ad 3. or mortal or venial, and from these she was free. Thus it is said: *you are fair, my beloved, and there is not a spot in you* (Song 4:7).
