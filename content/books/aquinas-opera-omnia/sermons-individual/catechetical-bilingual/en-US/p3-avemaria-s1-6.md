# AveMaria.S1.6

The first is because they are greater than men in dignity. For an angel is of a spiritual nature: *you make your angels spirits* (Ps 104 [103]:4). But, on the other hand, man is of a corruptible nature, for Abraham said: *I will speak to my Lord, whereas I am dust and ashes* (Gen 18:27). It was not fitting, therefore, for a spiritual and incorruptible creature to show reverence to one that is corruptible, as is a man.
