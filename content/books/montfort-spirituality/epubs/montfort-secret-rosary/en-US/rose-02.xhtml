Origin of the Rosary

Ever since St. Dominic established the devotion of the Holy Rosary, down to the time of Blessed Alan de la Roche, when it was re-established, it has always been called the Psalter of Jesus and Mary, because it has the same number of Angelic Salutations as there are psalms in the Psalter of David — that is, one hundred and fifty.

Since simple and uneducated people are not able to say the Psalms of David, the Rosary is held to be just as fruitful for them as David's Psalter is for others.
