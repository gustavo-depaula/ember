Chapter I

The Need for a True Devotion to Mary

Soul, living image of God and redeemed by the Precious Blood of Jesus Christ, the will of God for you is that you become holy like Him in this life, and glorious like Him in the next.

The acquisition of the holiness of God is your certain vocation; and this is what all your thoughts, words, and actions, your sufferings and movements of your life, must tend to; or else you are resisting God, by not doing that for which He created you and for which He is even now preserving you.

Oh! what a wonderful work! The dust changed into light, the unclean made clean, the sinful sanctified! This work, I acknowledge, is difficult in its nature; but by the grace and assistance of God, who gives it lavishly, it is easy. A thing done easily is a thing done well.
