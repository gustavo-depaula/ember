## Translation Decisions

- Latin passages: kept inline as in the original, since Montfort deliberately included them for the learned reader
- Editor footnotes: none present in this edition
- Montfort's rhetorical, passionate style preserved — long sentences kept where they reflect his oratorical rhythm
- "Dieu seul" kept as "God alone" at chapter endings
- Numbered paragraphs (1, 2, 3...) preserved exactly as in the source
- Rose numbering (1re Rose, 2e Rose...) rendered as "First Rose", "Second Rose", etc.
- "Dizaine" as chapter title rendered as "Decade" (First Decade, Second Decade, etc.)
