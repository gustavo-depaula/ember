# Psalms 116

*All nations are called upon to praise God for his mercy and truth.*

*(Does this psalm differ from what you were expecting? Don't be confused. There are two systems for arranging the psalms: the **Septuagint** and the **Masoretic**. We use the Septuagint system here. In the Masoretic system, which is used in most modern Bible translations, this psalm is called **Psalm 117**, and the **previous two psalms** are combined and known as **Psalm 116**.)*

*Alleluia.*

> **1** O Praise the Lord, all ye nations: praise him, all ye people.
> **2** For his mercy is confirmed upon us: and the truth of the Lord remaineth for ever.
