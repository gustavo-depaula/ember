# Station VII — Jesus Falls the Second Time

V). Adoramus te, Christe, et benedicimus tibi.

R). Quia per sanctam crucem tuam redemisti mundum.

Consider the second fall of Jesus under the cross, by which the pain of all the wounds of his venerable head and of all his other sacred members was renewed in the afflicted Lord. — Most gentle Jesus, how many times you have pardoned me, and how many times I have fallen again and offended you! By the merit of this new fall, give me the help to persevere in your grace until death. Grant that in all the temptations that assail me, I may always have recourse to you. I love you, Jesus my love, with all my heart. I repent of having offended you. Do not permit me to offend you again. Grant that I may always love you, and then do with me as you will.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be. Dear Jesus, etc.
