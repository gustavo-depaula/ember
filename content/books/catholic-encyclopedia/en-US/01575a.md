# Antiphellos

*Titular see of Lycia*

Now ANTEPHELO, or ANDIFILO, a titular see of Lycia, on the south coast of Asia Minor, at the head of a small bay; once suffragan of Myra. Little is known of its history.

## Sources

SMITH, *Dict. of Greek and Roman Geogr.*, I, 147; MAS LATRIE, *Trésor de Chronol.* (Paris, 1895), 1987.
