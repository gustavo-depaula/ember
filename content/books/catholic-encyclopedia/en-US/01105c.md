# Acmonia

*A titular see of Phrygia Pacatiana, in Asia Minor, now known as Ahat-Keui*

A titular see of Phrygia Pacatiana, in Asia Minor, now known as Ahat-Keui. It is mentioned by Cicero (Pro Flacco, 15) and was a point on the road between Dorylaeum and Philadelphia.

## Sources

SMITH, Dict. of Greek and Roman Geogr. (London, 1878), I, 21; MAS LATRIE, Tr sor de chronologie, etc. (Paris, 1887), 1979.
