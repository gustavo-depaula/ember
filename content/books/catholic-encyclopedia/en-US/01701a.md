# Areopolis

*Titular See of Palestine*

(Rabbath-Moab).

A titular see of Palestine. Its episcopal list (449-536) is given in Gams p. 454). There was another town of the same name in Lydia, Asia Minor.

## Sources

LEQUIEN, *Oriens Christ.* (1740), III, 536; SMITH, *Dict. of Greek and Roman Geogr.*, I, 197.
