# Prince Charles d'Aremberg

*Definitor-general and Commissary of the Capuchins (d. 1669)*

Definitor-general and Commissary of the Capuchins; died at Brussels, 5 June, 1669. He is the author of "Flores Seraphici", biographies of eminent Capuchins from 1525 to 1612 (Cologne and Antwerp, 2 vols., 1640) and "Clypeus Seraphicus" (Cologne, 1643), a defence of the "Annales Capucinorum" of Boverius.
