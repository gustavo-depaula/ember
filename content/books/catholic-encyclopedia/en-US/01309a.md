# Alfonso de Zamora

*A converted Spanish Rabbi, baptized 1506; died 1531*

A converted Spanish Rabbi, baptized 1506; died 1531. He revised the Hebrew text for Ximenes's Polyglot Bible, translated the Chaldee paraphrase in it, and added the sixth volume. He published also a work called "Introductiones Hebraicae" (Alcala, 1526).
