# Anastasiopolis

*Name of four ancient sees*

Name of four ancient episcopal sees located respectively in Galatia (suffragan of Ancyra), in Phrygia (suffragan of Laodicea), in Caria, and in Thraco (Gams, 441, 446, 448).

## Sources

MAS LATRIE, *Trésor de chronol.*(Paris, 1895), 1985: LEQUIEN, *Oriens Christ.*(1740) I, 485-486, 824-825, 913-914.
