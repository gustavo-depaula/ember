## Translation Decisions

- Numbered paragraphs: The source uses double numbering (e.g., "1. 1."). The first number is an absolute paragraph number across the whole book; the second is the chapter-relative number. Preserving this structure exactly.
- Latin quotations: Kept inline with their references, matching the original pattern. No separate translation provided unless the original does so.
- Scripture references: Preserved in brackets as in the original (e.g., [Lc 4,18]).
- "Monsieur Vincent" refers to St. Vincent de Paul — rendered as such with a translator's note on first occurrence.
- Register: Montfort writes in a direct, practical, legislative style here (these are rules, not devotional prose). Translation matches this: clear, dignified, somewhat formal.
- "Compagnie de Jésus" = Society of Jesus (Jesuits); "Compagnie de Monsieur Vincent" = Congregation of the Mission (Vincentians).
- "gaule" = switch/rod (for discipline of children in catechism context).
- "Providence" as a place name in ch. 7 (pratiques-missions §89) refers to a charitable dining hall for the poor, not the theological concept. Kept as "the Providence" with context making it clear.
