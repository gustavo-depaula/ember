# Part I — The Apostles' Creed

The first part of the Catechism unfolds the Apostles' Creed, the ancient summary of the faith the Church professes. Its twelve articles set forth what the Christian is to believe: the one God, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit; the creation of the world; the Incarnation, Passion, and Resurrection of Christ; the Holy Catholic Church; and the life everlasting.

The pastor is here instructed to teach not only the words of the Creed but their meaning, that the faithful may hold them with understanding and devotion.
