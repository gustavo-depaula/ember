## Pius X asks

**86.** What did Jesus Christ do in His earthly life?

*Jesus Christ, in His earthly life, taught us by example and by word to live according to God, and confirmed His doctrine by miracles; finally, to blot out sin, reconcile us with God, and reopen heaven for us, He offered Himself in sacrifice on the Cross, "the one Mediator between God and men."*

**87.** What is a miracle?

*A miracle is a perceptible event, above all the forces and laws of nature, and therefore such that it can come only from God, the Master of nature.*

**88.** By what miracles especially did Jesus Christ confirm His doctrine and prove that He is true God?

*Jesus Christ confirmed His doctrine and proved that He is true God especially by restoring in an instant sight to the blind, hearing to the deaf, speech to the mute, health to every kind of sick person, and life to the dead; by commanding as Master over demons and the forces of nature; and above all by His Resurrection from death.*

> **Scripture.** *Otherwise believe for the very works' sake.* — John 14:11

> *Master, when I doubt, let Your works carry me where my reasons cannot. They are still happening.*
