## Why the Precepts?

The precepts distill the Christian life into its sacramental rhythm: *Mass on the Lord's Day, Confession, Easter Communion, fasting, almsgiving, and the sanctity of marriage.* A Catholic who keeps them is living the bare shape of the faith. A Catholic who outgrows caring about them has usually outgrown the faith itself.

If you have fallen away from any of them, the path back is quiet and well-worn: go to confession. Every priest has heard it before, and none of it surprises Christ.
