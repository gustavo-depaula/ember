## Sacraments of Initiation

**Baptism.** The door of the Church and the gateway to the spiritual life. Baptism washes away original sin and every personal sin, incorporates the soul into Christ and his Body the Church, and bestows the indelible character of a child of God. *Once received, never repeated.*

**Confirmation.** The strengthening of the baptized by the gift of the Holy Spirit, sealing them more closely to Christ and equipping them to witness the faith in the world. *Once received, never repeated.*

**Eucharist.** The "source and summit of the Christian life" (CCC 1324). The Body, Blood, Soul, and Divinity of Jesus Christ, truly, really, and substantially present under the appearances of bread and wine. Received at Mass, and received often, it is the bread of pilgrims — the sacrament that sustains every other.
