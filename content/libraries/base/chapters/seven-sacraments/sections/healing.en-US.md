## Sacraments of Healing

**Penance (Confession, Reconciliation).** Christ's own mercy, spoken through the priest, restoring the soul to the friendship of God after sin. Serious (mortal) sin must be brought here before Holy Communion. Venial sin is remitted here too — and frequent confession is a great grace, even when one's sins seem small.

**Anointing of the Sick.** The oil and prayer of the Church over one gravely ill or near death, uniting the sufferer to the Passion of Christ, forgiving sin, and strengthening the soul for healing or for the final journey. It is not "Extreme Unction" for the dying only — ask a priest whenever illness is grave.
