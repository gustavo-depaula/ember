## Sacraments at the Service of Communion

**Holy Orders.** By which bishops, priests, and deacons are ordained to serve the Church in the name of Christ. Through the bishop the apostolic succession is preserved; through the priest, Mass is offered and sins are forgiven; through the deacon, the charity of the Church is extended. *A permanent character.*

**Matrimony.** The covenant by which a man and a woman give themselves to each other for the whole of life, for the good of the spouses and the procreation and education of children. Between two baptized persons it is a true sacrament, imaging the union of Christ and his Church.

Every sacrament is a meeting with the living Christ. Approach them often, worthily, with faith — and they will do what they promise.
