## Translation Decisions

- Numbered paragraphs (1, 2, 3...) preserved exactly as in the source, using **bold** formatting.
- Latin quotations kept inline with English translation following, matching the original's pattern.
- Editor footnotes: none present in the source text.
- Montfort's passionate, rhetorical style preserved — long sentences, exclamations, direct address to the reader.
- "Confrères" rendered as "Brothers" or "Fellow members" depending on context; Montfort addresses both men and women ("Frères et Soeurs").
- Archaic French expressions modernized only where necessary for comprehension.
- Scripture references preserved as-is.
- Verse/poetry rendered as blockquotes, matching the source.
