## Translation Decisions

- Latin quotations kept inline as in the original, with English translation following the same pattern Montfort uses (French gloss after Latin).
- Scripture references preserved in brackets as in the source.
- "Turcs" rendered as "Turks" (geographic/historical, not pejorative in context — Montfort means the Ottoman Muslims of his era). "Idolâtres" as "idolaters", "Juifs" as "Jews". These are standard 18th-century missionary language; no modernization needed here as they are not slurs.
- "Mahométans" does not appear in this text, so no modernization needed.
- "Liberos" — Montfort puns on the Latin word (children / free men). Preserved as Latin heading with English gloss in context.
- Numbered paragraphs preserved exactly as in the source.
- Rhetorical questions and exclamatory style preserved — this is a passionate prayer, not a treatise.
