Chapter III

Wonderful Effects of This Devotion

My dear brother, be sure that if you are faithful to the practices of this devotion which I shall point out, the following effects will take place in your soul:

By the light which the Holy Ghost will give you through Mary, His faithful Spouse, you will understand your own evil, your corruption, and your incapacity for anything good. In consequence of this knowledge, you will despise yourself. You will think of yourself only with horror.

The Blessed Virgin will also give you a share in her faith, which was the greatest of all faiths that ever were on earth. The soul in which Mary dwells will be visited by the Holy Ghost, and the Holy Ghost will form Jesus Christ in that soul.
