## Translation Decisions

- Latin quotations kept inline with English translation following, matching Montfort's own pattern of providing the French after the Latin.
- Scripture references preserved in their original format but normalized to colon notation (e.g., [Wis 7:14] instead of [Sg 7,14]).
- Montfort's numbered paragraphs preserved exactly.
- "les Turcs" modernized to "Muslims" in ch-14 (para 174) where it appeared alongside "Jews, pagans, heretics."
- No editor footnotes present in the source text; all footnotes are Montfort's own.
- Montfort's rhetorical, passionate tone preserved — long sentences and exclamations are characteristic of his style.
- Wisdom referred to as "she/her" when speaking of Eternal Wisdom personified (following the French feminine), but switching to "he/him" when the context clearly refers to Jesus Christ incarnate. This mirrors Montfort's own fluid use.
- "Bienheureux Henri de Suzo" rendered as "Blessed Henry Suso" (standard English name).
- "Ecclésiastique" rendered as "Ecclesiasticus" (Sirach) per traditional Catholic convention.
- Solomon's prayer (ch-15, para 191-192) translated from Montfort's French paraphrase of Wisdom 9, not from a standard English Bible.
