## Decisions Log

- **Footnotes**: All [^N] markers and footnote definitions dropped per instructions.
- **Latin quotations**: Kept inline in italics, as in the original. The author typically gives the Latin then an Italian gloss; we give the Latin then an English gloss.
- **Scripture references**: Preserved exactly as in source (e.g., "Io. XVI, 27", "Rom. VIII, 32").
- **"Capo I"** → "Chapter I" in headings.
- **Chapter subtitles**: Kept as the line after the heading, unformatted (as in source).
- **Blockquote sections**: "Affetti e preghiere" → "Affections and Prayers" as ## subheading.
- **Saints**: English standard forms throughout.
