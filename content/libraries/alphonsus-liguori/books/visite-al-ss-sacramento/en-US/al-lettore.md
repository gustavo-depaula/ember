# To the Reader

My dear reader, I beg you not to disdain this little book composed by me in a very simple style, since I have judged that in this way it may better serve the devotion of every sort of person.

Furthermore, I beg you to remember me before the Blessed Sacrament, whether I be dead or alive, each time you make use of it in visiting the Blessed Sacrament. And I promise to pray during the celebration of Holy Mass for everyone who does me this charity.
