# How to Practice This Holy Exercise

Each person, kneeling before the main altar, will make an act of contrition and form the intention of gaining these indulgences for himself or for the souls in Purgatory. He will say:

My Lord Jesus Christ, on this journey you went with such great love to die for me, and I have so many times turned my back on you; but now I love you with all my soul, and because I love you I repent with all my heart of having offended you. Forgive me, and permit me to come and accompany you on this journey. You go to die for love of me; I wish to come with you as well, to die for love of you, my beloved Redeemer. My Jesus, united with you I wish to live and die.
