# Station IV — Jesus Meets His Afflicted Mother

V). Adoramus te, Christe, et benedicimus tibi.

R). Quia per sanctam crucem tuam redemisti mundum.

Consider the meeting that took place on this journey between the Son and his Mother. Jesus and Mary looked upon each other, and their gazes became like so many arrows, piercing their loving Hearts. — Most beloved Jesus, by the sorrow you endured in this meeting, grant me the grace to be a true devotee of your Most Holy Mother. And you, my sorrowful Queen, obtain for me through your intercession a continual and loving remembrance of the Passion of your Son. I love you, Jesus my love; I repent of having offended you. Do not permit me to offend you again. Grant that I may love you, and then do with me as you will.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be. Dear Jesus, etc.
