## A Map of the Christian Soul

The Beatitudes are not a ladder to be climbed in sequence but a chord that sounds together in every holy life. Poverty of spirit grounds the rest; the purity of heart that sees God is the flower of them all.

> *"The Beatitudes reveal the goal of human existence, the ultimate end of human acts: God calls us to his own beatitude."* — CCC 1719

Pray with one Beatitude at a time. Ask which one Christ is inviting you to live today.
