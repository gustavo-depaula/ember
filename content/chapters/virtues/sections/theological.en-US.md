## The Theological Virtues

**Faith.** The virtue by which we believe in God and all that he has revealed and that the Church proposes for our belief. Faith is not feeling but a firm adherence of the mind to the Truth — who is God himself.

**Hope.** The virtue by which we desire the kingdom of heaven and eternal life as our happiness, trusting in Christ's promises and the help of the Holy Spirit. Hope keeps us from presumption and from despair.

**Charity.** The virtue by which we love God above all things for his own sake, and our neighbor as ourselves for the love of God. Charity is the "form" of every other virtue — they are truly virtues only when animated by love.

*"So faith, hope, love remain, these three; but the greatest of these is love."* — 1 Cor 13:13
