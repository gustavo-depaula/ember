Our Lord Jesus Christ made twelve promises to St. Margaret Mary Alacoque for those who practice devotion to His Sacred Heart. These promises, passed down through the tradition of the Church, encompass graces for every state of life — from peace in families to perseverance at the hour of death.

The promises are not magical guarantees but expressions of Christ's desire to pour out His grace upon those who open their hearts to Him through this devotion. They presuppose a sincere and persevering practice of the devotion, not mere mechanical observance.
