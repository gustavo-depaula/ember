## Text Sources & Licensing Notes

- **Public domain (pre-1930)**: look for existing digital editions on [CCEL](https://ccel.org), [New Advent](https://newadvent.org), [Project Gutenberg](https://gutenberg.org), [Internet Archive](https://archive.org)
- **Portuguese texts**: obras digitalizadas do dominio publico, Projeto Gutenberg PT, Internet Archive
- **Papal/Vatican documents**: generally freely reproducible per Vatican copyright policy
- **Modern works (post-1930)**: require publisher licensing agreements
- **Format**: we'll need consistent chapter/section markup for the reader engine; most public-domain sources will need cleanup
